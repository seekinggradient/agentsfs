# Granite Mutual

Insurer for homeowner's policy **HO-558210**. Claims line: 1-800-555-0140; portal handles filing and status but not disputes (those must go to the adjuster in writing).

- **Policy is replacement-cost (RCV), §4.2** — depreciation is recoverable after repairs are completed and receipts submitted. This is the lever in the current dispute; see [[status]].
- Deductible: $1,000. Water damage from sudden pipe failure: covered; gradual leaks: excluded (§6.1) — adjuster confirmed ours qualifies as sudden.
- Assigned adjuster: [[Dana Whitfield]].
